Your Zedjer Books guide

Use the top-edge search to open any section quickly. Your work is organised by company, so each company retains completely separate records.

1. Start with a company

Create or select a company from the Company dashboard. Set its financial year, currency and company details before posting entries.

2. Record transactions

Use New transaction to enter balanced debit and credit lines. Select the document type, document number, party, currency and narration.

3. Maintain accounts & parties

Chart of Accounts manages your ledgers and opening balances. Party Masters manages customers, suppliers and other contacts used in transactions.

4. Use reports

Reports includes Trial Balance, Financial Position, Income Statement, Cash Flow, Day Book, Transaction Register and General Ledger. Choose periods and export when needed.

5. Bank & Cash

Import bank statements, compare transactions and reconcile matches. Unreconciled statement entries can be converted into accounting transactions.

6. Master controls

Manage currencies, document numbering, users, activity logs, analysis setup, account subgroups and company profile details.

7. Backup safely

Company Backup & Restore downloads a password-encrypted .zdj backup. Restore always creates a separate company and does not overwrite an existing one.

8. Feature settings

Settings lets you simplify the workspace by switching optional areas on or off. Existing data remains intact while a feature is disabled.
